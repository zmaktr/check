ABOUT IATA TRAINING

IATA Training enables you to be the best you can be. Our 70+ years of experience in establishing aviation industry standards is directly integrated into our training, as our courses are developed and led by the very experts who set these standards.

Gain first-hand aviation insight and expertise. Develop your own professional skills, or train your organization/airline’s employees. Maintain compliance with high quality instruction that combines industry knowledge and certification on the latest regulations with practical application.
